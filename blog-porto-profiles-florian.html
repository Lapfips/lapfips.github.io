His past

Laval : 2006 - 2009

He was born in Laval where he lived for the first three years of his life with his parents and his two older sisters. But in 2009, after the birth of his third sister, his parents decided to move to Faverolles and he had to change school.

Faverolles : 2009 - 2012

Once he came to Faverolles he entered the school of the village from the first year of preschool (3 years old) to CM2 (9 years old). He skipped the CM1 class because the teacher was crazy and his mother asked the school to put him in a different class. But in 2013 his father found a job at Airbus in Blagnac, so they had to move to Blagnac.

Blagnac : 2013 - 2016

For his middle-school years, he went to Cours Molière, near the tramway station of Serventy-Airbus. This was a very good middle school with small class sizes. On this middle school every students had their own computer to work. He had very good results for his three first years there. But, during the forth year, his grades dropped down a little bit, and it was hard for him, but fortunately during the Covid-19 pandemic their was distance learning with videoconferencies. During this time, the school was much easier because he didn't have to go to school.

Blagnac : 2020 - 2023

He went to the high-school Sainte-Marie de Nevers, near the metro station of Jeanne-d'arc, just after his parents broke-up. Fortunatly he was at the same high-school as one of his friends and they were in the same class for their first year. He met new friends. He kept the same group until the end of high-school. During his second year he chose his specialities : Physics-Chimicals, Mathematics and Digital and Informatic Sciences. During this year he realized that he wanted to work in computer science. He was able to get his Baccalauréat with mention and with very good grades in his specialties.

Blagnac : 2023 - Today

During the last year of high-school, he received his letter of acceptance to the school he wanted to integrate: the IPI school. In september, he started his first year of college. Fortunately, the campus was near his house so he can go to the campus with his bike. He also met new people who have the same passion as him. He loves what he is doing in this school because he can work and learn more about informatic which always was his favorite discipline.
